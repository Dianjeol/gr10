Hi, I'm Helen

Spiritual, Trauma-Informed Coaching

I draw on formal training in ARCH Trauma-Informed Coaching, Inner Child Work with Paul Ferrini, and True Voice Work with Mark Fox, but most of all, my wisdom comes from life itself.

I have always been drawn to the deeper layers of life. From an early age, I asked big questions: not just to understand the world, but to find ways to bring healing into it. My path has been guided by a quiet but unwavering desire: to help people reconnect with their true selves, and to bring peace, within and without.

I believe that real change happens not through fixing or force, but through remembrance. My work is intuitive, trauma-informed, and rooted in deep empathy. I listen not just to words, but to what's underneath, honouring your pacing, history, and sacred potential.

I meet you with reverence, not strategy: inviting you back into connection with your own inner wisdom, creativity, and soul-led path.
