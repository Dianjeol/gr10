How We Work

Transformation and healing unfold most naturally when there is a sense of safety and being seen, and when we can listen inwardly to where opening feels possible.

The Witness Self

This work is most supportive when there is some capacity for what is often called the Witness Self — the ability to notice experience while remaining present with it, rather than being completely taken over.

We work to gently cultivate this capacity together. In times of deep trauma, it can feel inaccessible. Our shared aim is to slowly rebuild your trust in your own perception.

Meeting

My intention is to meet you exactly where you are — to offer a safe space of attentive listening, and to honour your life's journey up to this moment. We honour your survival patterns and defences as intelligent responses that once kept you safe.

Pace

Together, we can listen inwardly and sense whether this work feels like a good fit — and how we might proceed at a pace that honours your nervous system and sacred potential.

Sessions are co-created — shaped entirely by what you bring and what wants to move. No fixed programme. No timeline. Just you, and the process of coming home.
