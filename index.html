A Space to Land

To open to transformation and healing, we first need to feel safe and seen, sensing where we can gently open to our own vulnerability.

This begins with recognising a personal wish for change or healing. Each person's experience and nervous system responses are unique, and this first step calls for particular care and clarity.

My intention is to meet you where you are, offer a space of attentive listening, and explore together whether this work feels like a good fit, and how we might proceed.
