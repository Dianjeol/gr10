A Predictable Flow

1

Landing & Grounding

15 min

We begin by arriving. Before opening to story or emotion, we establish a sense of safety in the here and now — through orientation, gentle breathwork, or guided awareness to settle the nervous system.

2

Deep Listening & Inquiry

50 min

Once grounded, we gently turn toward your experience. We work with the Witness Self, moving only as fast as your body feels safe to go, and listening for the wisdom beneath the wounding.

3

Closing & Regulation

10 min

We conclude by gently returning to the present moment, ensuring you feel regulated and grounded before re-entering your day — integrating any insights discovered during the session.

Clarity & Care

In this work, clarity and boundaries are essential cues of safety. Naming the scope of our relationship helps prevent the boundary violations so often felt in high-control environments.

Our Shared Understanding

Unlike high-control systems that demand compliance, this space is built on mutual respect and transparency. Everything here is co-created — offered, never imposed.

What I Offer

  • › A non-judgmental, confidential space.
  • › Trauma-informed listening and somatic mirroring.
  • › A partnership in your journey of reclamation.
  • › Consistent pacing and clear communication.

What This Is Not

Not Clinical Psychotherapy: I do not diagnose or treat mental illness. This work is a supportive complement to, not a replacement for, clinical care.

Not Crisis Support: If you are in acute distress, please contact emergency services or a crisis line.
