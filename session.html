The Witness Self

This work is most supportive when there is some capacity for what is often called the Witness Self — the ability to notice experience while remaining present with it, rather than being completely taken over.

We gently cultivate this capacity together. In times of deep trauma, it can feel inaccessible. Our shared aim is to slowly rebuild your trust in your own perception.

Meeting

My intention is to meet you exactly where you are — to offer a safe space of attentive listening. We honour your survival patterns and defences as intelligent responses that once kept you safe.

Pace

Together, we sense whether this work feels like a good fit — and proceed at a pace that honours your nervous system and sacred potential.
