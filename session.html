How We Work

Transformation and healing unfold most naturally when there is a sense of safety and being seen.

Witness Self

We work to cultivate the capacity to notice our inner experiences while staying present with them, rather than feeling completely overtaken. In times of deep trauma, this capacity can feel inaccessible. Our work is to gently rebuild this trust in your own perception.

Meeting

My intention is to meet you exactly where you are, offering a safe space of attentive listening. We honour your survival patterns and defences as intelligent responses.

Pacing

Together, we can listen inwardly and proceed at a pace that honors your nervous system and sacred potential.
