Clarity & Care

Clarity and boundaries are essential cues of safety. Naming the scope of our relationship fosters a foundation of trust and honors the clear, supportive space where your autonomy can truly flourish.

Our Shared Understanding

Unlike high-control systems that demand compliance, this space is built on mutual respect and transparency. Everything is co-created: offered, never imposed.

What I Offer

  • › A non-judgmental, confidential space
  • › Trauma-informed listening and somatic mirroring
  • › A partnership in your journey of return
  • › Consistent pacing and clear communication

The Scope of This Work

A Complementary Space This work is a heart-led, non-clinical space for exploration. I do not diagnose or treat mental illness, and I encourage you to hold this coaching as a supportive complement to, not a replacement for, clinical care or psychotherapy.

Holding Safety While our sessions offer deep companionship, they are not designed for acute crisis support. In moments of immediate distress, I invite you to reach out to dedicated emergency services or a crisis line where you can receive the urgent, specialized care you deserve.
